About

TimE & Sp@cE

Welcome. I am Taiyzun Shabbir Shahpurwala, based in Bombay, India, with formative and working chapters across Dubai, London and New York. My practice works at two scales at once: inspect the record, understand the human context, identify what matters, then build the brief, map, workflow, register or communication that allows the work to move.

Among the meaningful chapters of my path was a period serving as Director of the Office of Dr. Huzaifa Khorakiwala, within a wider body of work connected with Wockhardt, Jupiter Events LLC, Wockhardt Foundation, the I Am Peacekeeper Movement, the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit, the EIRENE Awards and Billionaires for Peace. I hold that experience with gratitude; the relationships, standards and discretion shaped through it continue to inform the wider field.

Earlier work supplied the method. At STYLETEK, my father's principles met systems modernisation in garments and textiles: stock, suppliers, production and delivery had to agree. Gabbana Fashions refined my eye for fit and finish. A Wadia Group chapter deepened my work in websites, reporting and organisational workflows. Channel [V] India, Wizcraft and live production taught the other half of the lesson: when the signal is live, timing is part of the system.

International chapters carried the work through Dubai, the United States and the United Kingdom. From 2019 to 2023, work and learning across the United States moved through marine maintenance, job-development exposure, studio coordination, service and HR assignments, community support and creative research. London and Cheltenham sharpened public presentation, broadcast-support rhythm and team operations. The lasting education was practical: document the field, respect the handoff and stay calm while the moving parts change.

Across technology, media, cultural diplomacy, strategic communications and social innovation, the work is to make complex human, technical and commercial material understandable and decision-ready. It is Builder Mode: values turned into agreements, insight into architecture and experience into operating systems.

Each chapter now belongs to a wider creations archive, guided by seven operating values: Truth, Gratitude, Forgiveness, Love, Humility, Giving and Patience. The work must be precise enough for records, spacious enough for strategy and disciplined enough to protect trust, meaning and peace of mind.
